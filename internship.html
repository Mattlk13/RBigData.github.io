## Internship (Feb. 23, 2016) A 2.5 month 2016 summer internship (flexible dates) at the University of Tennessee (Oak Ridge National Laboratory campus) is available for a qualified student to develop big data infrastructure and applications in R. The internship includes a stipend for living, housing, and relocation. Required qualifications include strong knowledge of R and an interest in working with big data applications. Desired qualifications are some experience with software development and with parallel computing on large platforms. The student will be part of a team that develops infrastructure for and applications of the pbdR ecosystem of R packages. To apply, e-mail a CV and a paragraph describing why you are interested in the project to George Ostrouchov at go@tennessee.edu.

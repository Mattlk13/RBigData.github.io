FAQ

  • How hard is pbdR to install?
    The short answer is that it should mostly be as simple as issuing several install.packages() commands. The hardest part generally involves installing R and an MPI library. There are some special caveats to this for Windows users. See our installation page for full details about setting up a pbdR environment.
  • How do I get started with this?
    The best resource for getting started with this stuff is the pbdDEMO package, including its informative textbook-style vignette Speaking Serial R with a Parallel Accent. The tutorial page on this site offers some other good advice.
  • Where is pbdR installed and maintained?

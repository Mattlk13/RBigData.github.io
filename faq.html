## FAQ - [*How hard is pbdR to install?*](javascript:void(0)) The short answer is that it should mostly be as simple as issuing several `install.packages()` commands. The hardest part generally involves installing R and an MPI library. There are some special caveats to this for Windows users. See our [installation page](install.html) for full details about setting up a pbdR environment. - [*How do I get started with this?*](javascript:void(0)) The best resource for getting started with this stuff is the [pbdDEMO package](http://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/pbdDEMO/index.html), including its informative textbook-style vignette [Speaking Serial R with a Parallel Accent](http://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/pbdDEMO/vignettes/pbdDEMO-guide.pdf). The [tutorial page](tutorial.html) on this site offers some other good advice. - [*Where is pbdR installed and maintained?*](javascript:void(0)) pbdR is currently installed on the following HPC resources: <center> |UTK | ORNL | Other |----------------|-----------|------------------------ |Kraken (XSEDE) | Titan | Stampede, TACC (XSEDE) |Nautilus | Lens | tara, UMBC |Darter | Chester | Hopper, NERSC |Newton | Sith | Edison, NERSC </center /> Please contact us at Email at: **RBigData AT gmail** if you are interested in installing pbdR on your shared resources.

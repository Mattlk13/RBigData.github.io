## Overview The "Programming with Big Data in [R](http://www.r-project.org/)" project (pbdR) is a set of R packages for large scale, distributed computing and profiling. Our packages include high performance, high-level interfaces to [MPI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Message_Passing_Interface), [ScaLAPACK](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ScaLAPACK), [NetCDF4](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NetCDF), and more. While these libraries shine brightest on large distributed platforms, they also work rather well on small clusters and often, surprisingly, even on a laptop with only two cores. ## News (Jan 10, 2016) A Graduate Student Assistantship is available for a qualified student at the University of Tennessee to develop big data infrastructure and applications in R.

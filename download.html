## Stable We maintain stable distributions of all of our packages on the [Comprehensive R Archive Network (CRAN)](http://r-project.org/). For information about installing these packages, see our comprehensive set of [installation instructions](install.html). Additionally, you can find current binaries of our packages for the Windows operating system at our sister site, [HPSC](http://thirteen-01.stat.iastate.edu/snoweye/pbdr/?item=download). <center>
